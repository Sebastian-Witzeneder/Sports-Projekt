Our Opinion

We think most of the extreme sports are thrilling and we believe that you will have a lot of fun. Also we think it's an important topic to report about, because it seems to get more and more popularity. Why is it important to report about? And the Answer is, because if make a mistake you could lose your life. If you try it out and your guide makes a mistake he doesn't only risk his own life he also risks your life. But if the question is ask, if we would try it out our selfs our opinions are diffrent.

Down-Hill-Racing

Maybe you know Downhill racing if you like skiing, but that’s something different. For this type of sport, you need a mountain bike it’s kind of an equivalent for the summer. That means you must drive a course downhill, in the shortest possible time.

Ice-climbing

Ice-climbing is climbing ice formations such as frozen waterfalls and icicle.
It is the supreme discipline of alpine climbing as all alpine climbing knowledge is required.

Paragliding

In this type of sport, you are sitting in a harness.
You must navigate the parachute.
